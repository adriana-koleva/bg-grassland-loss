# bg-grassland-loss

## An Exploration of the Loss of Natural Grasslands in Bulgaria’s Protected Areas

### Overview

An exploration of the changes in land use in Bulgaria’s protected areas between 1990 and 2018, identifying the two protected areas with the greatest loss of natural grassland areas. 
These two protected areas are further examined in terms of several landscape descriptors aiming to evaluate the extent of landscape fragmentation.

### Study Area
Bulgaria's protected areas under the EC's Habitats Directive.

### Data
- Protected Area borders
- Corine Land Cover 1990 and 2018

### Software for Analysis
- ArcGIS Pro
- Microsoft Excel
